Fred, the Baker Donuts, is best known for fresh, glazed, yeast-raised doughnuts, especially the "Original Glazed," its first and best-known doughnut. All of its doughnuts are made from a secret recipe in the company since 1980. Fred retires and wants to keep Donut making alive with the Donut Maker.

Donut Maker was inspired by the game, Cookie Clicker. Cookie Clicker is a game about making an absurd amount of cookies. Cookie Clicker was originally released in 2013, but has been very actively developed since then. If you played it before, try it again to see all the new features!
